About Me

Hi!

I’m Lora Nabwala and I would best describe myself as a digital storyteller.

For as long as I can remember, I’ve been drawn to stories. It began with a love for reading that then blossomed into writing, photography, and graphic design. I’m fascinated by how each medium has its own way of telling a story and I enjoy exploring them. I'm always learning something new (like how to code this very site from scratch!), and I love diving into different creative niches. Most of all, I love hearing people’s stories and helping them tell theirs in the most meaningful way possible. Our stories connect us ; and I’d love to hear yours, too.
