Editorial Approach

I believe stories should center people and culture, not just the events themselves.

While my first love is writing, I carry my camera to every story, capturing moments that give words a visual anchor. My focus is culture and human-interest stories that reveal the resilience and depth of everyday people.

What sets me apart is how I merge photography with narrative and approach stories with a fresh, generational perspective.
